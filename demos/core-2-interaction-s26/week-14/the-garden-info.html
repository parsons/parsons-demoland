Exercise The Garden

You have a toolbox full of functions. You have a set of events. Your job is to wire them together and make a garden that feels like something.

The toolbox

Tool switching — selectFlower(), selectTree(), selectFence(), selectHouse(), selectStone(), selectMushroom()
Use placeCurrentTool(x, y) to plant whatever is selected.

Place directly — plantFlower(x, y), plantTree(x, y), buildFence(x, y), buildHouse(x, y), dropStone(x, y), plantMushroom(x, y)

Weather — rain(), sunshine(), nightfall(), dawn()

Transform everything — growAll(), shrinkAll(), wiltAll(), reviveAll(), scatterAll(), clearAll()

What's wired up

click — places the current tool.
keys 1–6 — switches between flower, tree, fence, house, stone, mushroom.
mousemove — empty.
timer 1 (3s) — empty.
timer 2 (10s) — empty.

The prompt

Give your garden a mood. When someone else opens it, they should feel something. Try wiring rain() to a timer, or growAll() to mousemove, or nightfall() to a key. The functions exist — you decide when they run.

Stretch

Use only original functions that you've written yourself.
