Randomness Random Color

No loop here — just Math.random(). Each time the page loads, three random numbers between 0 and 255 are generated and combined into an rgb() color. Reload the page to get a new color every time.

Math.random() returns a decimal between 0 and 1. Multiplying by 256 and rounding down with Math.floor() gives you a whole number in the range you need.
